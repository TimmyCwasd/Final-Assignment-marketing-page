Do you hate the hot, humid summer weather? Well, do we have the invention for you!

The breasy is an automatic cooling system that pulls the moisture from your home outside and fresh cool air inside. The breasy turns on automatically when it detects the home is hotter than 21 degrees celsius.

Prototype features

The breasy automatic home cooler has an LED system that displays the temperature of your home in degrees celsisus.
We have installed code onto the microbit to transmit the temperature in celsius from the sensor to the LED which displays that value.
